About Green View Guest House

Green View Guest House is located in Devalokam, Kottayam, a serene and peaceful area surrounded by lush greenery.
Away from the noise of the city, it offers a calm and relaxing environment for guests who wish to unwind and reconnect with nature.
Our guest house is ideal for families, pilgrims, and travelers looking for a comfortable stay in a quiet setting.
The surroundings are scenic and soothing, making it a perfect place for rest, reflection, and leisure.
With easy access to nearby town areas and local attractions, Green View Guest House combines convenience with tranquility.
We focus on providing a clean, welcoming, and homely atmosphere so that every guest feels relaxed and cared for during their stay.
