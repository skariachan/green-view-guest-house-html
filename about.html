About Us

Green View Guest House is located in the serene surroundings of Devalokam, Kottayam, Kerala. It is a peaceful retreat designed for guests who value comfort, privacy, and a relaxed atmosphere. We offer well-furnished rooms, a clean environment, and warm hospitality to make our guests feel at home. The calm surroundings and scenic views make it an ideal place to unwind and rejuvenate. Whether you are traveling with family, visiting nearby places of worship, or exploring Kottayam, Green View Guest House provides a comfortable and welcoming stay.
